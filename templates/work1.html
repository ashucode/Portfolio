001 : Environment Monitoring System

Pollution Data was captured using a custom self-made monitoring box. After taking data from local repository to server, data was analysed. A paper was also published in IEEE on this research

"Analyzing Correlation Between Air and Noise Pollution with Influence on Air Quality Prediction"

A custom circuit box was made comprising the pollutant sensors wired with arduino board. Data captured in the arduino board was thne transferred to the local repository in the raspberry pi which was also in the box. For Data transmission, we hosted an online portAL where live data generated was shown, could be downloaded also as csv file. Another mode of data transmission was a bluetooth module attached in the pi which when any paired system came closer and connected, the data would be transmitted.

Data was retrieved from three kind of sources, housing, busy road, and industrial, to gather a variety of data.Using python library matplotlib,numpy, we visualized the pollutant's nature with respect to the area. The Datasheet was then trained in ML algorithms(Scikit Learn) as Decision Tree, K-nearest neighbors, and Random Forest, and based on the accuracy we got, it showed the influence of noise pollution in air quality prediction. Another point of view can be obtainned from the research is that based on the data, we could verify the source of the pollution

This research was published in 2018 IEEE International Conference on Data Mining Workshops (ICDMW) You can find our paper here. It was presented in Singapore on 17-20 Nov,2018
